Pizza

Pizza: The Universal Comfort Food

Pizza, originally from Italy, has become a global phenomenon known for its simplicity, versatility, and mouthwatering flavor combinations.

Over time, pizza has evolved into countless regional variations, from the thin and crispy Neapolitan style to the deep-dish pies of Chicago

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup fat-free milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl; stir in milk and olive oil until a soft dough forms.
  3. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead 10 times. Shape dough into a ball; cover with an inverted bowl and let sit for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle on a baking sheet.
  5. Add your favorite toppings and bake. Enjoy!
