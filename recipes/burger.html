Burger

Burger: The Icon of Modern Fast Food

The burger is a quintessential fast food staple, known for its combination of a juicy patty, soft bun, and a variety of toppings. Typically made with ground beef, though often substituted with chicken, turkey, or plant-based alternatives, the burger is usually grilled or fried to perfection

Despite its fast-food origins, the burger has transcended into gourmet territory, with chefs around the world elevating the dish by using high-quality ingredients, unique toppings, and artisanal buns.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
